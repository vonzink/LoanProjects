Form 1120 Corporation Income Calculator

Calculate monthly income from C-corporation (Form 1120) returns with ownership percentages and comprehensive add-backs.

Instructions: This calculator derives the qualifying income for a C‑corporation (Form 1120) by summing the listed lines, subtracting certain expenses, multiplying by your ownership percentage and deducting dividends received. For lines labelled "+/–" please enter a negative value if the item represents a loss. The final income used will follow the standard averaging rule: if two years are entered and the most recent year shows higher income, the two years are averaged over 24 months; otherwise the most recent year is divided by 12.

Calculation Policy: This calculator replicates the 1120 worksheet logic. It sums the selected lines for each year, subtracts total tax, short‑term debt and meal & entertainment exclusions, multiplies by your ownership percentage and reduces dividends paid to you. If a second year is entered and Current Year income exceeds Prior Year, the two years are averaged over 24 months; otherwise the most recent year is divided by 12. Results are rounded to two decimal places.
