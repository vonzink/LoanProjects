1120S K-1 Income Calculator

Calculate monthly income from 1120S K-1 S-corporation distributions with support for multiple K-1s.

Instructions: Use this calculator to compute qualifying income for K‑1s issued by S corporations (Form 1120S). Enter amounts for each K‑1 you have. If multiple K‑1s exist, complete each section. The calculator will sum the ordinary business income or loss, rental income or loss and other rental income or loss for each year. If the most recent year is higher and a second year is provided, the two years are averaged over 24 months; otherwise the most recent year is divided by 12.
