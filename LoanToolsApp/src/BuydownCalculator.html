How 3-2-1 Buydowns Work

  • Current Year: Interest rate is reduced by 3% from the note rate
  • Prior Year: Interest rate is reduced by 2% from the note rate
  • Year 3: Interest rate is reduced by 1% from the note rate
  • Full Payment: Interest rate returns to the full note rate
  • Funding: Typically paid by seller, lender, or borrower as a credit at closing
  • Benefits: Lower initial payments help borrowers qualify and reduce early payment shock
